Introduction

This project explores the Nobel Prize dataset through the lens of data science. The Nobel Prize is not just a record of individual achievementit reflects broader patterns of global leadership, academic focus, gender dynamics, and historical events. By analyzing this dataset, we can uncover trends that speak to the evolution of science, peace efforts, and cultural recognition over time.

Why This Topic Matters

This analysis is relevant both generally and within the field of data science. The Nobel Prize helps define who and what societies value. For data analysts, it offers a rich real-world dataset with imperfections, gaps, and structureperfect for building practical skills in data wrangling, visualization, and storytelling.
